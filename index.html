About BeatBuddy

BeatBuddy is a Discord bot that provides music from YouTube to your voice channels.

Originally developed in Python as a simple, partially-functional bot in December of 2021, the bot was torn down and built from the ground up using Node.js and Discord.js throughout February of 2022.

At this time, BeatBuddy is locally hosted and has a handful of both general commands and music related commands. However, futher development will be taking place in the near future in order to add:

  • 24/7 Bot Activity
  • Playlist and LiveStream Support
  • Spotify and SoundCloud Support
  • More specific and in depth commands
  • Permissions Checking
  • Roles Assignment

So stay tuned for updates on the site and enjoy your time with BeatBuddy!

List of Commands

The prefix for each command is "//"
Example:

User: //ping
BeatBuddy: pong

General Commands:

  • ping -- Responds with "pong"
  • hello (aliases: hey, hi) -- Reply's with "Hey ..." to the user who sent the command
  • clear int (aliases: delete, del) -- Clears the specified integer of messages up to 14 days old
  • help -- sends a list of all available commands

Music Related Commands:

  • play [search or url] (aliases: p) -- Plays the requested song in the voice channel if nothing else is playing, otherwise adds the song to the queue
  • skip (aliases: next) -- Skips to the next song in the queue
  • pause -- Pauses the current song
  • resume (aliases: unpause) -- Resumes the song if paused
  • join -- Joins the voice channel without audio until the play command is invoked
  • leave (aliases: stop) -- Leaves the voice channel and clears the song queue
